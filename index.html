Crime Scene Cleanup

Our team provides professional, discreet, and fully certified crime scene cleanup services. We handle hazardous situations with precision, ensuring safety and compliance with all local and federal regulations.

    Services include:

  • Cleanup of blood and bodily fluids
  • Removal and safe disposal of biohazardous waste
  • Handling infectious and blood-borne pathogens
  • Trauma and accidental death scene restoration
  • Disposal of hazardous materials

All work is performed by trained and equipped professionals, prioritizing the safety of clients, the public, and our staff. We ensure that every scene is thoroughly sanitized and restored, giving peace of mind and a safe environment after a traumatic event.

Disaster Cleanup

Services include:

Our disaster cleanup services provide fast, thorough, and safe restoration after unexpected events. Whether it’s hurricane damage, fire, flooding, or severe storms, our team is trained to handle the situation efficiently, minimizing further damage and ensuring a safe environment.

  • Water damage cleanup and structural drying from floods or burst pipes
  • Fire and smoke damage restoration
  • Hurricane damage cleanup, including debris removal and property stabilization
  • Mold inspection and removal after water intrusion
  • Debris removal and hazardous material handling
  • Full sanitization and restoration of affected areas

We combine professional-grade equipment, certified techniques, and experienced personnel to restore properties quickly and safely, helping clients recover from disasters with confidence and peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How eco-friendly are your cleaning products and methods?

All our cleaning solutions are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and safe for families, pets, and the environment. We prioritize sustainability without compromising on cleanliness or effectiveness.

2. What should I do to prepare my space before a cleaning appointment?

To help our team work efficiently and deliver the best results, please remove personal items, fragile decorations, and any clutter that may block access to surfaces. This helps our team clean efficiently and thoroughly.

3. What is your cancellation or rescheduling policy?

We request at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or reschedules. This allows us to accommodate other clients and adjust our schedule without penalty.

4. Are your cleaners bonded and insured?

Yes! Our team is fully bonded and insured, giving you peace of mind that your property is protected. We are proud participants in the Federal Bonding Program, which provides employment opportunities for ex-offenders. While bonded workers are a vital part of our team, there are certain jobs they cannot perform—such as work inside federal buildings or other restricted facilities. In those cases, we assign other qualified staff to ensure compliance, while still providing meaningful opportunities for all our bonded workers wherever possible.

5. Do you offer recurring cleaning services or one-time appointments?

We offer both! Whether you need a one-time deep clean, move-in/move-out service, or regular weekly or monthly maintenance, we can customize a plan that fits your needs and schedule.
